OUR WORK

The PWD Road, stretching from Gate No. 1 to Gate No. 2, has undergone a thorough cleaning, including the back lanes. What is even more commendable is that this cleaning initiative will now be implemented regularly! We owe a debt of gratitude to the diligent efforts of the PWD team, as they have ensured that the road remains consistently clean and well-maintained, guaranteeing a smooth and enjoyable journey for everyone. Let us applaud their unwavering commitment to cleanliness and join hands in preserving the beauty of our community by keeping it free from litter. Together, we can create a cleaner and more appealing environment for all.
